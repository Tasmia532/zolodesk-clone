Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zolodesk?

Zolodesk is a comprehensive support help desk platform designed to streamline customer service operations. By offering a centralized system for ticket generation and management, Zolodesk ensures that your clients can easily reach out for support, fostering a more efficient and organized approach to customer care.

With Zolodesk, businesses can create and customize fields to tailor the support experience to their unique needs. The platform also allows for the formation of specialized agent groups, ensuring that tickets are assigned based on expertise for faster and more effective resolution. This targeted approach enhances both customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Additionally, Zolodesk offers the capability to design personalized email templates, improving communication with customers. The platform also supports the registration and management of multiple companies or sub-accounts, providing a versatile solution for businesses of all sizes. Zolodesk is your all-in-one tool for delivering exceptional customer support.

Is Zolodesk free software?

Zolodesk offers a 14-day free trial for you to explore its features and determine if it’s the right fit for your needs. During this trial period, you can experience all the functionalities of Zolodesk without any cost, allowing you to make an informed decision before committing to a subscription.

Does Zolodesk provide 24/7 support?

Zolodesk provides 24/7 support to ensure that you have assistance whenever you need it. Our dedicated support team is always available to help you resolve any issues or answer any questions, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience with our platform.
